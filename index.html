HackPS is an AI system that answers:
“Which problem statement are we built to win?”

We treat a team as a system. Skill overlap and past project signals are combined to form a team synergy profile.

Hackathon problem statements are then analyzed for domain requirements, complexity, and time risk to assign a probability of successful execution.

What makes the system different is explainability. Every recommendation comes with a clear breakdown, allowing teams to make strategic decisions.
